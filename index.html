Meet your teacher

Hi, I’m Andrea.

Growing up, music was my trusty sidekick. As the oldest of six kids and with frequent moves, I often felt unanchored. Everything changed when I was nine and our family got a second-hand piano.

With lessons, I dove into playing hymns, radio hits, Broadway tunes, and the pieces assigned by my piano teacher. Today, music binds my family of five kids together, and I love sharing that same joy with students.

Teaching since 2014

I have been a piano teacher in the Folsom / El Dorado Hills area since 2014 and want every student’s musical journey to be full of joy and creativity.

The Harmony Station approach

Whole-body learning with heart.
